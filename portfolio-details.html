SAP ERP System Rollout - COR3

Shoprite, one of the largest supermarket chains in Africa, announced in 2017 that they would be rolling out a new SAP Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system across their operations. This new system would replace their existing legacy systems and would help streamline their business processes, improve efficiency, and enhance customer service. The SAP ERP system would cover all aspects of the Shoprite business, including procurement, inventory management, financial accounting, human resources, and customer relationship management. The system would be implemented in phases, starting with the company's operations in South Africa and then expanding to its stores in other African countries. The implementation of the SAP ERP system was a significant investment for Shoprite, but the company believed that it would provide significant long-term benefits, including improved data analytics capabilities, better supply chain management, and greater visibility into business operations. The rollout of the system was expected to take several years to complete, with ongoing support and maintenance provided by SAP.
